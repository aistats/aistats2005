Accommodation in Barbados

 

The workshop will be held at The Savannah Hotel in Barbados.

 

Alternative accomodation is at the Blue Horizon. Shuttle bus service will be provided between Blue Horizon and the Savannah.

 

Conference Rates:


Savannah - Single Occupancy $210
Savannah - Double Occupancy $225
Blue Horizon - Single Occupancy $125
Blue Horizon - Double Occupancy $135

Conference rates are per night in US dollars and include a continental breakfast. Reservations should be arranged with the hotel using this hotel registration form. After the hotel reservations deadline rooms are subject to availability.

Other nearby hotels which might have available rooms include the Amaryllis Hotel (\$253.70 per night) and The Grand Barbados Hotel (\$207.63 per night).

Hotel Reservations Deadline: November 19, 2004
